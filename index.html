About-us

Established in 1995 our company is a family bussines, started by my father who was providing construction and renovation services of all sorts in Sweden and Scandinavia. In 1999 My brother and I have joined my fathers company and together we expanded rapidly. Completing bigger and more complicated houses designs in Sweden, Danmark, Uk, Poland and Canada over time we were growing and evolving to get framing and construction projects ahead simultaneously remaning in renovation business. We are a highly-qualified profesional construction and renovation team, with over forty years of experience in building field known for our service quality as well as time of completing our projects.
